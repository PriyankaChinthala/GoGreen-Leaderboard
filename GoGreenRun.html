How It Works

Your Mission

Complete eco-friendly activities at the GoGreen Kiosk during the run and earn points to climb the leaderboard!

Activities & Points

  • Already a member / Become a member (20 points)
    Show your membership or sign up at the kiosk
  • Green Trivia (max 20 points)
    Answer green trivia questions (1-20 points based on performance)
  • Carrying water bottle to the run (15 points)
    Bring your own reusable water bottle
  • Collect Green Stamps (15 points)
    Earn stamps for eco-friendly actions
  • Take Green Pictures (10 points)
    Snap a photo at the GoGreen photo booth
  • Get your colleague to sign up (20 points)
    Refer a colleague to join GoGreen

Rewards

Top scorers will be featured in the upcoming GoGreen Newsletter! Your contribution to sustainability will be celebrated across the organization.
